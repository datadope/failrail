Running In Circles Service disruptions on newer rail lines

POSTED: 18 August 2013

SMRT's Circle Line is the newest rail line in Singapore. The Circle Line was opened in stages since 2009 and it became fully operational in 2011.

In Minister Lui's parliamentary reply on 13 August 2013, he stated that on the Circle Line, there were only three service disruptions of more than 30 minutes each year from 2010 to 2012. In 2013, there was none so far.
